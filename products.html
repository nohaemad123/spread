Our Products

Our products have been installed in many distinguished governmental and private projects in Arab Republic of Egypt.

Ceiling Diffuser CD

Can be supply or return, square or rectangular shape with one, two, three or four ways patterns.Diffuser core is fully removable.

Linear Slot Diffuser LSD

There are three models of LSD supply, return or dummy decorative, available in 1 to 8 slots, width of slot 1" or 3/4". Available in curved or corner shapes.

Plenum Box

Is designed to ensure equal distribution of air through the linear slot diffusers. There is acoustic lining in the supply plenum box to reduce noise and turbulence generated in the duct before reaching to the grill.

Linear Bar Grill (LBG)

LBG Comes in many models, Supply, bar and blade, bar and register, core only, frame without flange and floor grill.

Air Grill & Register

Air Grills & Registers come in many models: Supply air grill, supply air register, return air grill and return air register with horizontal, vertical blades or combined

Ceiling Round Diffuser CRD

Can be used as supply or exhaust in cooling, heating or ventilation application with fully adjustable cores to provide horizontal and vertical air pattern, removable inner core permit easy installation.

Fresh Air Louver FAL

Used for exhaust and fresh air is composed of horizontal and fixed blades and inclined down 450 and spaced which allows air to pass through it while keeping out un wanted elements such as water, dirt etc.

Sand Trap Louver STL

Sand Trap Louver are very effective in trapping sand while having attractive look, it is constructed from two sets of inverted C-Channels mounted and vertically and self-emptying holes at the bottom.

Volume Control Damper

Is made of single or multi blades, hand looking quadrant is provided for manual operation, extended shaft and motor base is provided for motorized operation, it can be rectangular or circular, opposed blades action on request.

Disk Valve DV

Is manufactured from high quality steel and polyester powder coated, center core is adjustable by screw and nut for air control, the outer frame is fitted with a foam rubber gasket.

Fire Damper Curtain Type FD

This model is approved for use in wall, floor and partitions resistance rating of 3 hours or more. It is designed to stop the spread of fire throughout ventilation system, it is fitted with fusible link 72 0C (165 0F)

Jet Diffuser

Jet Diffuser is designed in areas where a long throw is needed. Inner rings can be controlled in both horizontal and vertical directions. One, two, three, four jet diffuser elements per panel can be selected.
